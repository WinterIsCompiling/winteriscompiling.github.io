Privacy Policy

This policy describes how Winter Is Compiling treats your information.

Information we collect on our website

winteriscompiling.uk uses Google Analytics, a website analysis service provided by Google. This service uses "cookies", small text files stored on your device, to collect anonymised standard visitor information provided to us by your browser. This information (including the visitor's IP address) is transmitted to and stored by Google, and is used to provide statistical reports of user activity on winteriscompiling.uk.

For more information on how Google handle your data, please see Google's Privacy Policy.

For more information on cookies, please see AllAboutCookies.

Information we collect through TaddyPole

Winter Is Compiling does not collect any user information through TaddyPole. Any information entered into the game is stored on the device the game was played on, and is never transmitted to any other device or service.
